Privacy Policy for Sunset Cafe

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Sunset Cafe values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Sunset Cafe mobile and web application collects, uses, and protects information when customers, staff, and administrators use the service.

Information we collect

  • Phone number used for OTP-based sign-in or verification
  • Email address used for admin or staff login
  • Table identifier and session details for dine-in ordering
  • Order information, including selected menu items and quantities
  • Service requests submitted through the app
  • Technical data required to operate the app and maintain security

How we use information

  • To authenticate users and verify sign-in attempts
  • To place and manage cafe orders
  • To support staff and admin access controls
  • To process service requests from customers
  • To improve app reliability, security, and support

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers or technical infrastructure used to operate the app, such as authentication, messaging, hosting, or database services, and only when necessary to provide the service.

Data retention

We retain information only for as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect user data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your choices

You may contact us to request updates or deletion of your information, subject to applicable legal and operational requirements.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age required by local law without parental involvement.
